The Story of Gin & More

Created by Francesco Mattài Del Moro

In autumn 2023, a simple gin tasting gift sparked a dream. Francesco designed the logo and imagined a niche, artisanal gin from his Italian homeland — one made in small batches with passion and meaning.

Gratitude

The first distillate features pink pepper, false pepper, and Artemisia — the miraculous herb. This edition is dedicated to Fausto, a dear person who always hoped for a miracle. The very first bottle was made in his memory.

Friendship

The second recipe adds Moringa — known as the "tree of miracles" — with origins in India and Pakistan. Brought back by his close friend Jacopo after a trip to India, it gives the gin a fresh, summery character.

Love

The latest creation is dedicated to Francesco’s children — Giacomo, Costanza, and Filippo. Inspired daily by their unique spirits, he added birch lymph, celebrated for its purifying properties and known as the "lymph of life."

The Logo

Designed by Francesco himself, the two Ms represent his surname Mattài Del Moro, while the heart-shaped D symbolizes the deep love binding his family origins.
