The Story of Gin & More

Created by Francesco Mattài Del Moro

In autumn 2023, a gin tasting gift sparked a dream. Francesco designed the logo and began crafting a niche artisanal gin from his Italian homeland — small-batch and deeply personal.

Gratitude

The first distillate features pink pepper, false pepper, and sacred Artemisia. Dedicated to Fausto, whose wish for a miracle inspired it all.

Friendship

Enhanced with Moringa — the "tree of miracles" — brought from India by close friend Jacopo.

Love

Dedicated to Francesco’s children — Giacomo, Costanza, and Filippo — with purifying birch lymph, the "lymph of life."

The Logo

Two Ms for Mattài Del Moro, connected by a heart-shaped D representing family love.
