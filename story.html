The Story of Gin & More

Created by Francesco Mattài Del Moro

In autumn 2023, a gin tasting gift sparked a dream. Francesco designed the logo and crafted a niche artisanal gin from his Italian homeland — small-batch, meaningful, and full of soul.

Gratitude

Featuring pink pepper, false pepper, and sacred Artemisia. Dedicated to Fausto, whose hope for a miracle inspired the first bottle.

Friendship

Infused with Moringa, the "tree of miracles," gifted by friend Jacopo from India.

Love

Dedicated to Francesco’s children — Giacomo, Costanza, and Filippo — with purifying birch lymph, the "lymph of life."

The Logo

Two Ms for Mattài Del Moro, connected by a heart-shaped D symbolizing family love.
